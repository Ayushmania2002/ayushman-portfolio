Research Profile

Plant biologist with expertise in molecular genetics, plant–microbe interactions, and computational structural biology. Currently serving as Senior Research Fellow at ICAR-IARI, New Delhi, contributing computational and molecular biology insights to an ICAR-funded genome-editing project aimed at engineering climate-resilient rice. MS dissertation focused on characterising the transcription factor CYCLOPS in Arachis hypogaea (groundnut) and mapping its downstream interactors in the root nodule symbiosis (RNS) signalling network, employing site-directed mutagenesis, RNAi-based mutant generation, hairy root transformation, confocal and electron microscopy, molecular docking, and in silico structural analyses.
